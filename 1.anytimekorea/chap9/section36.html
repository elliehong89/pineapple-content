6. 잠시만요. “Just a second, please.”

잠시만요 “Just a second, please.” is used when you ask the listener to wait for a moment. 잠시 literally means “a short time” and 만 means “only.” For the same purpose, you can also say 잠시 기다리세요 “Please wait a moment.” or 잠시만 기다리세요 “Please wait just a moment,” using the verb 기다리다 “to wait.”

1.
A:
신분증 있으세요?
Do you have your identification card? [신분증 ID card]
B:
네. 잠시만요.
Yes. Just a second.
2.
A:
물 좀 주세요.
Please give me some water.
B:
네. 잠시만 기다리세요.
Sure. Please wait just a moment.
3.
A:
샘플 좀 보여주세요.
Please show me a sample.
B:
잠시만요.
Just one moment, please.

Note1

You can also say 잠깐 or 잠깐만 instead of 잠시 or 잠시만, respectively.

잠깐만요

One moment, please.

잠깐 기다리세요

Please wait a moment

잠깐만 기다리세요

Please wait just a second
