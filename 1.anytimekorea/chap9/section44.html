Following the directions below, act as Brian and Jennifer, and create a conversation

1. Ask how many brothers and sisters Brian/Jennifer has and where they live.

2. Ask where his/her parents live

3. Ask whether Brian/Jennifer has grandparents. Ask he/she where they live.

4. You may ask more questions on your own.
