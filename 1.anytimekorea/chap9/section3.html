Note1

형제 can mean either “siblings” or “brothers” depending on the context. Based on your gender, a different word is used for siblings. If you are a female speaker, use 언니 for older sister and 오빠 for older brother. If you are a male speaker, 누나 is used for older sister and 형 is used for older brother. Refer to Lesson 9 (III. Culture, p.251) for more information.

Note2

동생 means “younger sibling.” 남동생 is “younger brother” and 여동생 is “younger sister.” 남 and 여 came from the words 남자 “men” and 여자 “women,” respectively. Refer to Lesson 9 (III. Culture, p.251) for more information.

Note3

다음 주 is a combination of 다음 “next” and 주 “week.” You can also remember 이번 주 “this week” and 지난 주 “last week.”

Note4

“집” means “house.” Depending on the context, 집 can mean either the house where you live now or your hometown.
