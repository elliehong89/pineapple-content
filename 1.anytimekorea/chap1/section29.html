VI. Spontaneous Conversation

Now is a chance to have a realistic interaction with your classmates in Korean. You can practice speaking with your classmates, writing, and presenting in front of the class!

STEP 1.

Meet three classmates and ask each of their names. Act out and create a meaningful conversation just as you practiced in the Guided Conversation.

Following the guidelines below, talk to each other creatively.

1. Greet each other.

2. Ask their names.

3. Clear up a misunderstanding about your name's pronunciation.

4. Say that you are glad to meet them.

STEP 2.

Complete the table with the information you collected from your three classmates.

이름 / 성함
Classmate 1
Classmate 2
Classmate 3

STEP 3.

Introduce your three classmates in front of the class. While introducing them, you may point each person out with an appropriate gesture and say their names.

For example,

이 친구는 (this friend) (Classmate 1's name) 이에요/예요.

그리고 (and) 저 친구는 (that friend) (Classmate 2's name) 이에요/예요.

그리고 (and) 이 친구는 (this friend) (Classmate 3's name) 이에요/예요
