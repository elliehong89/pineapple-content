VI. Spontaneous Conversation

Now is a chance to have a realistic interaction with your classmates in Korean. You can practice speaking with your classmates, writing, and presenting in front of the class!

STEP 1.

Meet with three classmates and ask his/her name and nationality. Please act out and create a meaningful conversation just as you practiced in the Guided Conversation.

Talk to each other creatively, following the given guidelines.

1. Greet each other.

2. Ask your partner’s name and nationality.

3. Exchange pleasantries

STEP 2.

Complete the table with the information you collected from your three classmates.

이름 / 성함 국적 (nationality)
Classmate 1
Classmate 2
Classmate 3

STEP 3.

Introduce your three classmates in front of the class. While introducing them, you may point out to each person with an appropriate gesture and say their names.

For example,

이 친구는 (Classmate 1’s name) 이에요/예요. (nationality) 사람이에요. 그리고

저 친구는 (Classmate 2’s name) 이에요/예요. (nationality) 사람이에요. 그리고

이 친구는 (Classmate 3’s name) 이에요/예요. (nationality) 사람이에요.
