VI. Spontaneous Conversation

Now is the chance to have a realistic interaction with your classmates in Korean. You can practice speaking with your classmates, writing, and presenting in front of the class!

STEP 1.

Talk to three classmates one at a time, and ask where they live, the size of their room, and the household items available. Please act out and create a meaningful conversation just as you practiced in the Guided Conversation.

Try to make your conversation flow naturally, following the given guidelines.

1. Ask one’s residence type (e.g, 기숙사; 아파트; 주택 house)

2. Ask one’s preference of the residence.

3. Ask the size of the room.

4. Ask about available household items

5. Try to include 커요; 작아요 (be small); 넓어요 (be spacious; be wide); 좁아요 (be cramped; be narrow) in the conversation.

6. Try to include 책상; 책장; 의자 (chair); 거실 (living room); 소파; 오븐; 침대; 전자레인지 in the conversation.

7. If you can, use some other expressions from previous lessons to make your conversation creative.
