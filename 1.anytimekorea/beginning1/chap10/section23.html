4. ~을까요?/~ㄹ까요? “Shall I/we~?; Do you think ~?”

Usage

Use ~을까요?/~ㄹ까요? to suggest to the listener to do something together or to ask about the listener’s opinion or preference.

Form

Use the rule below to choose between 을까요? and ~ㄹ까요?

▶ Verb/Adjective (ending in a consonant) + 을까요?

:

먹을까요? [ㄱ + 을까요]

1.
뭐 먹을까요?
What should we eat?
2.
불고기 먹을까요?
Should we eat bulgogi?

▶ Verb/Adjective (ending in a vowel) + ㄹ까요?

:

갈까요? [ㅏ + ㄹ까요]

* Verb/Adjective (ending in ㄹ) + 까요?

:

만들까요? [ㄹ + 까요]

1.
이제 갈까요?
Should we go now?
2.
뭐 살까요?
What should we buy?
3.
불고기 몇 파운드 살까요?
How many pounds of bulgogi should we buy?
4.
햇반 몇 개 살까요?
How many packs of instant rice should we buy?
5.
몇 명 올까요?
How many people do you think will come?
6.
마크 씨가 모임에 올까요?
Do you think Mark will come to our gathering?
7.
무슨 영화 볼까요?
What movie should we watch?
8.
제가 할까요?
Should I do it?
9.
토요일에 만날까요?
Should we meet on Saturday?
10.
무슨 음식 만들까요?
What food shall we cook?
11.
마이클 집이 학교에서 멀까요?
Would Michael’s home be far from the school?
