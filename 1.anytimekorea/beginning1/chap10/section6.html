1. Native-Korean and Sino-Korean numerals (beyond 10)

Note1

Native Korean numerals are used for numbers 1 to 99. From number 100, only SinoKorean numerals are used.

Note2

The following is some examples of how you would read both numerals under 100. For example, to read 34, combine 30 + 4 as in 서른넷 (native Korean) or 삼십사 (Sino -Korean). Here are some examples of how you will read the numbers.
