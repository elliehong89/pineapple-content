V. Guided Conversation

Keeping in mind the organization of the model conversation in Listen & Discuss (Script), practice the modified conversation below with your partner.

Situation 1:

Two friends are at a coffee shop in Koreatown in New York. They are about to order drinks.

STEP 1.

Practice the following conversation with your partner, using the information in ( ).

  1. 01
    A:

    (B's name)

    씨,
    뭐

    (드실래요)(마실래요)

    ?
  2. 02
    B:
    저는

    (아메리카노요)(녹차요)(커피요)(오렌지주스요)

    .

    (A’s name)

    씨는요?
  3. 03
    A:

    (저는) (저도)

    (아메리카노요)(녹차요)(커피요)(오렌지주스요)

    .
  4. 04
    B:

    (Name the drinks you want to order)

    ,제가 살게요.
  5. 05
    A:
    아녜요! 아녜요. 오늘은 제가 살게요.

    (B’s name)

    하나 주세요.
  6. 06
    B:
    네~. 그럼 다음에는 제가 살게요~
  7. 07
    A:
    네, 그러세요~ ㅎㅎ 제가 주문할게요.
    (A speaks to the café worker next to him/her.)
    Choose (1) or (2) to continue the conversation.
  8. 08
    A:
    (1)

    (Drink name 1)

    하나하고

    (Drink name 2)

    하나 주세요.
    (2)

    (Drink name)

    둘 주세요.

(Switch roles and continue to practice.)

STEP 2.

Act out and present the above conversation just as you practiced in Step 1 in front of the class. Try not to read it!
