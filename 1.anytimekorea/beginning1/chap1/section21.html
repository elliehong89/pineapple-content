4. 뭐예요? “What is it?”

You just learned “이름이 뭐예요?” (What is your name?). “뭐예요?” on its own means “What is (it)?”, which is a combination of “뭐” (what) and “예요” (is).

(Korean)

(Referring to something) 뭐예요?

(Literal translation)

(Referring to something) What is?

Look at the following examples and see how 뭐예요? is used.

1.

B: 뭐예요?

What is it?

A: 포토 앨범이에요.

It’s a photo album.

2.

B: 뭐예요?

What is it?

A: 초콜릿 쿠키에요.

It’s a chocolate cookie.
