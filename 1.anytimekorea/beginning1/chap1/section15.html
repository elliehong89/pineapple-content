Exercise 7

Your Korean friend is asking you your favorite actor/actress or singer. Name your favorite celebrity in Korean, using ~이요/~요. And then write his or her name in the blank.

1.

Your friend : 좋아하는 배우가 누구예요?

You :

Who is your favorite actor/actress?

2.

Your friend : 좋아하는 가수가 누구예요?

You :

Who is your favorite singer?
