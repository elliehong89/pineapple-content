6. Useful expressions in daily conversation

The following is a list of useful expressions that are commonly used in daily conversation. Listen to the audio and repeat after each sentence.
100

Korean
English translation
1.
안녕하세요?
How are you?/Hi.
2.
안녕히 가세요.

Good-bye. (to someone leaving)

[Lit., Please go in peace.]

3.
안녕히 계세요.

Good-bye. (to someone staying)

[Lit., Please stay in peace.]

4.
감사합니다.
Thank you.
5.

고마워요.

고맙습니다.

(I) appreciate it. [informal]

Thank you. [formal]

6.
뭘요!
No problem! (You are welcome.)
7.
아니에요.
It’s nothing. (You are welcome.)
8.
별말씀을요.

Don’t mention it.

[Lit., Don’t mention it. (You are welcome.)]

9.

미안해요.

미안합니다.

I’m sorry. [informal]

I’m sorry. [formal]

10.

죄송해요.

죄송합니다.

I’m truly sorry. [formal]

11.

괜찮아요.

괜찮습니다.

That’s fine./I’m okay. [formal]
