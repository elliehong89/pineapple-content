2. Vowels

2.1 Simple vowels

The following are the most widely used eight simple vowels in Korean. The sound of each vowel letter is similar to that of the bolded English letter(s).

ㅏ far

ㅓ up

ㅗ ohio

ㅡ book

ㅣ tree

ㅐ fan

ㅔ pen
