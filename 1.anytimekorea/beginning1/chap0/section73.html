Pronouncing two consonants on the bottom in a syllable that are followed by a vowel

In two consonants, both consonants are pronounced. The first consonant is not released, whereas the second consonant is carried over to the next syllable and pronounced together with the vowels.

Example :

몫이 → 목시 ; 읽어요 → 일거요 ; 맑아요 → 말가요 ; 앉아요 →안자요 여덟이 → 여덜비 ; 값이 → 갑시 ; 닮았다 → 달맜다
