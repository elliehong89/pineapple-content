3. Consonants

3.1 Simple consonants

There are nine simple consonant letters. The first five letters (ㄱ, ㄴ, ㅁ, ㅅ, ㅇ) were based on the shape of speech organs. The other four letters (ㄷ, ㄹ, ㅂ, ㅈ) were derived from ㄴ, ㄱ, ㅁ, and ㅅ. The sound of each consonant letter is similar to that of the bolded English letter(s) below.

ㄱ gun

ㄴ nose

ㄷ door

ㄹ roll
