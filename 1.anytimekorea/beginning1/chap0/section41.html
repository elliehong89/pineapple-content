3.2 Air-puffing consonants

When additional strokes are added to the five letters ㄱ, ㄷ, ㅂ, ㅇ and ㅈ, the “h”-like sound is added to create air-puffing sounds (aspiration) ㅋ, ㅌ, ㅍ, ㅎ and ㅊ. The sound of each consonant letter is similar to that of the bolded English letter(s) below.

ㄴ nose
