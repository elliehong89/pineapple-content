5. “Subject-Object-Verb” (SOV) word order

Some verbs take an object. In these cases, Korean follows the word "Subject-Object-Verb" (SOV) order. In the SOV sentences, it is common to omit the subject when it is understood among the speakers through context.

Look at the following examples and see the difference in the word order between the two languages.

1.

린다는 한국어를 공부해요.

공부해요 study Verb

(SOV)

Linda Subject

(SOV)
