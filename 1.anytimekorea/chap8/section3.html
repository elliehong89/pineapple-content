Note1

아녜요, literally meaning "(It) is not," is the shortened form of 아니에요. It can be used as a response to 감사합니다 or 고마워요 "Thank you." In that case, 아니에요 or 아녜요 means "Don't mention it." or "No problem." or "You're welcome."
