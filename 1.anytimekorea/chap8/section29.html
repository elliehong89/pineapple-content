VI. Spontaneous Conversation

Now is a chance to have a realistic interaction with your classmates in Korean. You can practice speaking with your classmates, writing, and presenting in front of the class!

STEP 1.

A waitress is about to take your order at your table. You and your classmate are discussing drinks and food. Please act out and create a meaningful conversation just as you practiced in the Guided Conversation.

Follow the directions 1-3. If you can, use other expressions from previous lessons to make your conversation flow naturally.

1. Discuss you and your classmate’s preference for drinks and food.

2. Offer to treat your classmate this time.

3. Place an order to the waiter

STEP 2.

Using the information in Step 1, write a narrative about what you and your classmate ordered.

For example,

저는(name of your order) 먹어요/마셔요. (name of classmate 1)는/은 (name of his/her order) 먹어요/마셔요.

STEP 3.

Based on what you wrote in Step 2, present in front of the class what you and your classmate ordered. Try not to read what you wrote in Step 2!
