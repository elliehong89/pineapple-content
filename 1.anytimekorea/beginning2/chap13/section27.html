VI. Spontaneous Conversation

Now is the chance to have a realistic interaction with your classmates in Korean. You can practice speaking with your classmates, writing, and presenting in front of the class!

STEP 1.

Using the family trees of you (A) and your classmate (B) below, talk about each other’s family members in terms of their age, birthday (month & day), and how they look. Make sure to include the expressions from the box below in your conversation.

STEP 2.

Write a narrative that introduces your classmate’s family members and their age, date of birth and more.

Example: (Name of your classmate) 씨 아버지는 연세가 쉰둘(52)이세요. 그런데, 사십 대 (his forties)로 보이세요. 생신은 오 월 (May) 십삼 일 (13)이세요.
