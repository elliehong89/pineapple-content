2. Verb base + ~으러 가다/~러 가다 “to go for the purpose of ~”

Usage

Use “Verb base + ~으러 가다/~러 가다” to express the purpose of going. It means “to go in order to do ~” or “to go for the purpose of doing ~.” You may put some other information between 으러/러 and 가다, which is indicated with (...) below.

Verb base + 으러/러 (...) 가다/오다 to go for the purpose of ~

to go for the purpose of ~

Form

Use the rule below to choose between ~으러 (...) 가다 and ~러 (...) 가다.

▶ Consonant-ending verb base + 으러 (...) 가다/오다

▶ Vowel-ending verb base + 러 (...) 가다/오다

* Verb base (ending in ㄹ) + 러 (...) 가다/오다
