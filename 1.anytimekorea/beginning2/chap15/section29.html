With the model conversation in Listen & Discuss (Script) in mind, practice the modified conversation using the food items below with your partner.

  • 짜장면

  • 짬뽕

  • 스테이크

  • 햄버거

  • 핫도그

STEP 1.

It’s getting close to meal time and two friends are trying to decide what to eat. Practice the following conversation with your partner, choosing one of the options in ( ) to make the conversation flow naturally.

STEP 2.

Present the conversation from Step 1 in front of the class. Try not to read it!
