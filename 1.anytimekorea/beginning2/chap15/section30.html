VI. Spontaneous Conversation

Now is the chance to have a realistic interaction with your classmates in Korean. You can practice speaking with your classmates, writing, and presenting in front of the class!

STEP 1.

You and your friend are going to have dinner tonight. Share a conversation about your dinner plans with your partner following the instructions below.

1. Please include the following information in your dinner planning conversation.

ㆍ What food you/your friend like

ㆍ Which restaurant you/your friend have been to

ㆍ What dish you/your friend recommend

ㆍ What dish you/your friend recommend

ㆍ What dish you/your friend plan to order at the restaurant

ㆍ The price of the dishes you/your friend plan to order at the restaurant

1. Please include the following information in your dinner planning conversation.

ㆍ How was the dish you/your friend ordered?

ㆍ Why (or Why not) did you/your friend like the restaurant?

STEP 2.

Complete the table below, based on the conversation you had in “Step 1.”

STEP 3.

Based on the table you completed in Step 2, write a simple review of the restaurant you and your friend visited.

For example: 오늘 저녁 먹으러 (restaurant name)에 가 봤어요. 친구는 (dish name)을 주문했어 요. 저는 (dish name 1)를 좋아하는데, 친구가 (dish name 2)을/를 추천했어요. 그래서, (dish name 2)를 주문했는데, 맛이 없었어요. 가격은 ($$)정도 했어요. (restaurant name)에 가면, (dish name 2)은/는 주문하지 마세요. (dish name 1)을/를 주문하세요.

useful expressions

추천하다

가격

STEP 4.

Based on what you wrote in Step 3, present about the restaurant and the dishes in front of the class. Try not to read what you wrote in Step 3!
