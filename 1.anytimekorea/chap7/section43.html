VI. Spontaneous Conversation

Now is a chance to have a realistic interaction with your classmates in Korean. You can practice speaking with your classmates, writing, and presenting in front of the class!

STEP 1.

Talk to three classmates about their cellphone numbers and write them down. After you ask them, your partner will ask yours. While talking to each other, act out and create a meaningful conversation as you practiced in the Guided Conversation!

Try to make your conversation flow naturally, following the guidelines below.

1. Ask their cellphone numbers.

2. Use 다시 한 번요; “다시 한 번 말씀해 주세요” when needed.
