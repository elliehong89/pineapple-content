Note1

“됐어요.” literally means “became.” Depending on the contexts, it can mean various things including “It’s done,” “It’s okay,” “It’s enough,” “Fair enough,” “It has worked out,” and “No, thanks.”

Note2

“다시 한 번요.” means “One more time, please.” If you wish to say “Please tell me one more time,” you may say “다시 한 번 말씀해 주세요.”
