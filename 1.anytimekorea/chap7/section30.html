5. -은/는/이/가 어떻게 되세요? “May I have ~?”

"-은/는/이/가 어떻게 되세요?" is an indirect question of -"은/는/이/가 뭐예요?"

전화번호가
phone number
뭐예요?
what is
What is (your) phone number?
(Direct question)
전화번호가
phone number
어떻게 되세요?
how become
May I have your Phone number?
(Indirect question, therefore more formal)

Note1

Here are common expressions using -은/ -는/ -이/ -가 어떻게 되세요?

성함은/이 어떻게 되세요?

May I have your name?

주소는/가 어떻게 되세요?

May I have your address?

이메일은/이 어떻게 되세요?

May I have your email?

생년월일은/이 어떻게 되세요?

May I have your date of birth?

신용카드 번호는/가 어떻게 되세요?

May I have your credit card number?
