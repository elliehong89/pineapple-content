3. Make a request or suggestion using ~으세요/~세요.

Use ~으세요/~세요 in commanding/requesting/suggesting sentences to show your courtesy to the listener or to make your request less direct.

여기 보세요.

Please look here.

빨리 오세요.

Please come quickly.

이리 오세요.

Please come this way

앉으세요.

Please sit down.

공부하세요.

Please study.

운동하세요.

Please exercise.

여기 보세요

Please look here.

시작하세요.

Please begin.

여기 보세요

Please look here.

샌드위치(를) 드세요.

Please have some sandwich.

이거 드세요.

Please eat this. [이거 this]

읽으세요.

Please read.
