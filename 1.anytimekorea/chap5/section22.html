4. ... 잘하시겠어요. “You must be good at ... .”

“... 잘하시겠어요” literally means “(You) must do ... well.” It means “You must be good at ... .”

1.
A:
저는 수영 선생님이에요.
I’m a swimming instructor.
B:
수영(을) 잘하시겠어요.
You must be good at swimming.
2.
A:
저는 셰프예요.
I am a chef.
B:
그래요? 요리(를) 잘하시겠어요.
Really? You must be good at cooking.
3.
A:
저는 프로 게이머예요.
I am a professional gamer.
B:
정말요? 게임(을) 잘하시겠어요.
Really? You must be good at gaming.
4.
A:
저는 보컬 트레이너예요.
I am a vocal trainer.
B:
그래요? 노래(를) 잘하시겠어요.
Really? You must be good at singing.

useful expressions

요리
cooking
노래
song
수영
swimming
