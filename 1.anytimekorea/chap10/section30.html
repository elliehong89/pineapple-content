5. 그렇게 ~? “that (much)~?”

그렇게 literally means “like that.” But when it is used to emphasize the following phrase, the meaning changes to “that much.”

Use the rule below to choose between 을까요? and ~ㄹ까요?

1.
그렇게 바빠요?
Should we go now?
2.
그렇게 좋아요?
Is it that good?
3.
그렇게 비싸요?
Is it that expensive?
4.
그렇게 재미있어요?
Is it that fun?
5.
방이 그렇게 작아요?
Is the room that small?
6.
숙제가 그렇게 많아요?
Do you have that much homework?
7.
A:
매일 커피 다섯 잔 마셔요
I drink five cups coffee everyday.
B:
그렇게 많이 일해요?
Do you work that much?
8.
A:
저는 매일 한국 음식 먹어요.
I eat Korean food everyday.
B:
그렇게 자주 먹어요?
Do you eat that often? [자주 often]
