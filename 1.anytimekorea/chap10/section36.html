III. Culture

English loan words that are frequently used in Korean

People count Korean money with Sino-Korean numerals, such as 일, 이, 삼, 사…. Korean money is colorful and each bill shows a significant figure in history or notable cultural artifacts of Korea.

For counting weights or distances, Koreans use the standard metric system similarly to Europe and other Asian countries. For example, when you go to a grocery store, meats are weighed and sold in grams or kilograms. The highway signs show distances in kilometers. Some commonly used measurements are shown below.

g 그램
kg 킬로그램
cm 센티미터
m 미터
km 킬로미터
