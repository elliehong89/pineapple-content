家 가 house/-ist

Combination of house (宀) and pig (豕), which gives birth to many babies, so the character came to mean a family. It is a play on words for fertility. Used also as an ending for someone in a specific profession, like "-ist" in pianist.

Character Pronunciation Meaning
家 가 [ga] house, -ist, family

Examples

  • 가족 Ga-jok (家族) house/family + difficult = family
  • 가난 ga-nan (家難) house/family + difficult = poverty
  • 가구 ga-gu (家具) house + organize/tool = furniture
  • 가장 ga-jang (家長) house/family + adult/leader = breadwinner
  • 국가 guk-kka (國家) country + house = nation
