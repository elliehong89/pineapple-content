1. A은/는 B이/가 아니에요. “A am/are/is not B.”

meaning

In Lesson 2, you’ve learned “A은/는 B이에요/예요.” which means “Someone is NOUN” or “Something is NOUN.” The negative sentence of “A은/는 B이에요/예요.” is “A은/는 B 이/가 아니에요.” which means “Someone/Something is not NOUN.”

Usage

Use the rule below to choose between -이 아니에요 and -가 아니에요.
